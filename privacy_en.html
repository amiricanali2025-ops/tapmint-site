TapMint Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the TapMint mobile app (“the App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) may collect the Advertising ID and device data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • The App itself does not actively collect personally identifying information such as your name, address, or email.

2. Purposes of Use

  • Ad delivery and effectiveness measurement
  • Service operation (point management, daily caps, etc.)
  • Detection and prevention of fraud and abuse

3. Third-Party Sharing

Within the above scope, ad networks and anti-fraud partners may receive data necessary for ad delivery and security.

4. Your Choices

You can reset the device’s advertising ID or disable personalized ads in your OS settings.

5. Security

We take appropriate and reasonable safeguards to protect data to the extent practicable.

6. Children

The App is not directed to children under the applicable age in your region. If you are a guardian and believe a child provided data, please contact us.
